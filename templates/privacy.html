Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

1. Introduction

Spendly is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our expense tracking service. We believe in transparency and giving you control over your financial data.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, and password when you create an account.
  • Expense Data: Transaction details you enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the service, such as login times and feature usage.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the expense tracking functionality.
  • To personalize your experience and display your expense history.
  • To send you service-related notifications (e.g., account confirmations).
  • To improve our service and fix bugs or issues.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may use trusted third-party services (such as hosting providers) to operate our service, but these parties are bound by confidentiality agreements and do not have independent rights to your data.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience, remember your preferences, and analyze site traffic. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal data at any time.
  • Correct inaccurate information in your account.
  • Delete your account and all associated data.
  • Export your expense data for personal use.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date above.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through the support channels provided within the application.
